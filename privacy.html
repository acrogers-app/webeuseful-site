Most of Our Apps — No Data Collected

For the majority of our apps, we respect your privacy completely:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed.
  • Any data used in the app stays on your device.

This applies to: Useful Reminder, We Are Focused: Pomodoro Timer, RLFGC, and other apps by Webeuseful unless otherwise noted below.

Voto: Live Polls — Full Privacy Details

Voto is a group polling app that stores data on remote servers to enable real-time shared experiences. This section fully describes what we collect, why, and your rights.

⚠️ Important: Unlike our other apps, Voto stores poll content and account data on Firebase servers — not solely on your device. Please read this section carefully.

What We Collect

DataWhyWhere Stored
Apple ID identifierTo identify your account and link polls to youFirebase Firestore
Display name (if shared via Apple)Shown as poll creator nameFirebase Firestore
Poll content (titles, options, notes)Core app functionalityFirebase Firestore
Vote selectionsTo display real-time resultsFirebase Firestore
Voter name (optional, if poll requires it)Shown to poll creator onlyFirebase Firestore
Device push tokenTo send poll notificationsFirebase Firestore
Language preferenceDisplay app in your languageOn device only
Crash & diagnostic dataBug fixes and stabilityFirebase (anonymous)

We do NOT collect: real email addresses (Apple hides these), location, contacts, browsing history, health data, or financial information. We never sell your data to third parties. No advertising. No profiling.

Sign In With Apple

Voto uses Sign In With Apple. Apple provides us an anonymized user identifier — we never receive your real Apple ID email unless you explicitly choose to share it. Apple manages your credentials; we never store your password.

Content Moderation & Administrator Access

Voto administrators can view poll content stored in our database for safety and abuse prevention purposes. We may review content when a poll is reported, when automated systems flag potentially harmful content, or when required by law. Content violating our Terms of Service will be removed. For illegal content involving minors, we will report to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperate fully with law enforcement.

Your Rights GDPR / CCPA

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Deletion — delete your account and all data in-app (Profile → Delete Account) or by emailing us
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability — request your data in portable format
  • Opt-out of notifications — iOS Settings → Notifications → Voto

To exercise these rights email allen.webeuseful@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Voto is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Contact us immediately if you believe a child under 13 has an account.
