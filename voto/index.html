Voto – Live Polls

Create a poll in seconds, share a link with anyone, and watch votes roll in live — no app required to vote.

Download on the App Store

Create

  • Build a poll in seconds with up to 6 options.
  • Use templates: Yes/No, A vs B, Rate 1–5, and more.
  • Set an auto‑close date or close manually anytime.
  • Require voter names and allow comments for richer feedback.

Share

  • One link — anyone can vote from any device, no app needed.
  • Share via Messages, WhatsApp, email, or any platform.
  • Remind voters with a built‑in nudge when time is running low.

Watch

  • Results update live in real time.
  • See vote counts, percentages, and the leading option instantly.
  • Full voter breakdown with names and comments.

Perfect for

  • Teachers collecting class votes and feedback.
  • Teams making fast product and scheduling decisions.
  • Families planning vacations, meals, and weekends.
  • Event planners, coaches, and community organizers.
