Useful Location Reminder

Never forget errands again. Useful Location Reminder lets you attach reminders to places, not times — so you’ll be pinged when you actually arrive at the store, office, or gym.

Download on the App Store

Remember things in the right place

  • Set reminders to trigger when you arrive at or leave a location.
  • Use saved places for home, work, grocery, pharmacy, and more.
  • Add notes so you remember exactly what you needed to do.

Smart, respectful alerts

  • Choose arrival, departure, or both.
  • Control how often reminders repeat, so you’re nudged but not nagged.
  • Works in the background with minimal battery impact.

Perfect for

  • Grocery and errands lists.
  • Remembering to pick up or drop off items when you’re nearby.
  • Location-based todo items for work and school.
