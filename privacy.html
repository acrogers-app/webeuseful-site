App Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy.

Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed.
  • Any data used in the app stays on your device.

Voto: Live Polls — Additional Privacy Information

Voto allows users to create and share polls. Please note:

  • Polls and votes are stored securely in Firebase Firestore.
  • No personally identifiable information is required to vote.
  • Poll creators may optionally sign in — no profile data is shared with third parties.
  • Vote data is only used to display real‑time poll results.
  • Firebase may collect anonymous diagnostic data per their own privacy policy.

You can review Firebase's privacy policy at:
firebase.google.com/support/privacy
